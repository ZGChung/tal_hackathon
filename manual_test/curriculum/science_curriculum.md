# Science Curriculum

## Overview
This comprehensive science curriculum emphasizes inquiry-based learning, scientific method, and real-world applications across physical, life, and earth sciences.

## Core Disciplines

### Physical Sciences

#### Physics
- Motion and forces
- Energy and work
- Waves and sound
- Light and optics
- Electricity and magnetism
- Modern physics

#### Chemistry
- Atomic structure
- Chemical bonding
- Chemical reactions
- Acids and bases
- Organic chemistry
- Environmental chemistry

### Life Sciences

#### Biology
- Cell biology
- Genetics and heredity
- Evolution
- Ecology
- Human anatomy and physiology
- Plant biology

#### Health Sciences
- Nutrition
- Disease prevention
- Public health
- Medical research

### Earth Sciences

#### Geology
- Earth's structure
- Plate tectonics
- Rocks and minerals
- Geological processes

#### Meteorology
- Weather patterns
- Climate science
- Atmospheric science

#### Astronomy
- Solar system
- Stars and galaxies
- Space exploration
- Cosmology

## Scientific Method
1. Observation and questioning
2. Hypothesis formation
3. Experimentation
4. Data collection and analysis
5. Conclusion and communication

## Key Skills
- Scientific inquiry
- Laboratory techniques
- Data analysis
- Critical thinking
- Scientific communication

## Cross-Cutting Concepts
- Patterns
- Cause and effect
- Systems and system models
- Energy and matter
- Structure and function
- Stability and change

## Real-World Applications
- Environmental science
- Technology and innovation
- Medical advances
- Sustainable practices
- Space exploration
