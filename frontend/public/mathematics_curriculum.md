# Mathematics Curriculum

## Overview
This curriculum covers fundamental mathematics concepts for K-12 education, focusing on building strong mathematical foundations and problem-solving skills.

## Grade Levels

### Elementary School (Grades K-5)

#### Number Sense
- Counting and cardinality
- Place value
- Addition and subtraction
- Multiplication and division
- Fractions and decimals

#### Geometry
- Shapes and their properties
- Spatial reasoning
- Measurement (length, area, volume)
- Coordinate systems

### Middle School (Grades 6-8)

#### Algebra
- Linear equations
- Quadratic equations
- Polynomials
- Functions and graphs
- Systems of equations

#### Geometry
- Triangles and their properties
- Circles and their properties
- Area and perimeter
- Volume and surface area
- Transformations

#### Statistics and Probability
- Data collection and analysis
- Mean, median, mode
- Probability concepts
- Basic statistical inference

### High School (Grades 9-12)

#### Advanced Algebra
- Polynomial functions
- Rational functions
- Exponential and logarithmic functions
- Sequences and series

#### Trigonometry
- Trigonometric functions
- Trigonometric identities
- Solving trigonometric equations
- Applications of trigonometry

#### Calculus
- Limits and continuity
- Derivatives
- Integrals
- Applications of calculus

## Key Concepts

### Problem Solving
- Mathematical reasoning
- Critical thinking
- Pattern recognition
- Logical deduction

### Mathematical Communication
- Expressing mathematical ideas
- Using mathematical notation
- Explaining solutions
- Justifying reasoning

## Assessment Methods
- Formative assessments
- Summative assessments
- Problem-solving tasks
- Mathematical projects

## Resources
- Textbooks and workbooks
- Online learning platforms
- Mathematical software
- Manipulatives and tools
